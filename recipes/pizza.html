Funghi Pizza

Delicious pie ideal for fun guys!

Funghi pizza is pizza topped with a variety of different fungi. Don't worry, they won't take you for any trips though.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (best if you make it)
  • Alfredo sauce is my go-to for funghi pizza
  • Cheese...do you consider it pizza if there is no cheese?
  • Mushrooms. The ones from behind your house will do

Making the Pie

  1. Make your dough into a shape. Circles are standard, try a sphere if you're feeling gutsy.
  2. Evenly spread the sauce over your dough.
  3. Sprinkle the cheese over the sauce.
  4. Add those mushrooms from under the tree in your backyard and TADA!! you've done it!
  5. Make sure your oven is at 425 degrees and place the pizza in the middle rack for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Remove and serve while hot.
